About TrekFast

TrekFast helps adventure lovers discover, plan, and book unforgettable trekking experiences. From peaceful nature walks to challenging mountain trails, we make every journey safe, simple, and exciting.

Our Journey

TrekFast officially launched on 1 January 2026 with a vision to make trekking accessible to everyone — beginners, families, solo travelers, and adventure enthusiasts.

We noticed that planning a trek was often confusing, time-consuming, and risky. So we built a platform where users can easily explore verified treks, compare options, and book with confidence.

Today, TrekFast is growing into a trusted hub for outdoor adventures across India, helping thousands reconnect with nature.
